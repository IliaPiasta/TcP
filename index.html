    Frequently Asked Qusetions

    The google command allows you to perform a search on Google directly from Discord. Just enter the command /google [search query], and the bot will provide you with a link to the search results.
    The /video command helps find videos based on the query you provide. Enter /video [keywords], and the bot will find the relevant video and provide a link to it.
    The /warn command is used to issue a warning to a user. It is useful in situations where a moderator wants to notify a participant about rule violations without taking more severe actions.
